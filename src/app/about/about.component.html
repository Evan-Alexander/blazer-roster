About the Portland TrailBlazers

The Portland TrailBlazers play for the city of Portland, Oregon. Sadly, the Blazers haven't won a championship since 1977. They currently have one of the youngest rosters in the league with lots of potential to mature. With the addition of Jusuf Nurkic, the team has currently turned itself around. Hopefully, in the off season they'll pick up at least one solid first round pick out of their three! Also, here's hoping said pick isn't plagued with Portland's history of drafting an injury prone player.
